### The Pennsylvania Paradox

**Higher residential rates, competitive commercial rates‚Äîwhy?**

1. **Transmission Costs:** Pennsylvania's residential rates include higher transmission charges to move power from western generation to eastern load centers
2. **Wholesale Competition:** Commercial and industrial customers can negotiate directly with generators
3. **Time-of-Use:** Large customers access lower off-peak rates
4. **Generation Surplus:** Pennsylvania generates more than it consumes, exporting to neighboring states
